Returns & Refunds

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you can return it within 30 days of delivery.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must:

  • Be unused and in the same condition as received
  • Have all original tags attached
  • Be in original packaging
  • Be accompanied by the invoice/receipt

How to Initiate a Return

1

Login to Your Account

Go to your account and navigate to the Orders section

2

Select the Item

Choose the item you want to return and click "Return Item"

3

Choose Return Reason

Select a reason for return and add any additional comments

4

Schedule Pickup

Choose a convenient date and time for pickup, or drop at a partner location

5

Receive Refund

Once we receive and verify the item, your refund will be processed

Alternative: Email us at surajnandan78@gmail.com with your order details to initiate a return.

Refund Timeline

Once we receive your return, we will inspect the item and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

Processing Time

5-7 business days after receiving the item

UPI / Wallets

1-2 business days after approval

Credit/Debit Cards

5-7 business days after approval

Net Banking

5-7 business days after approval

COD Orders

Bank transfer within 7-10 business days

Note: Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (wrong or defective item).

Exchanges

We offer free exchanges for size or color within 30 days of delivery, subject to availability.

Exchange Process

  1. Initiate a return request selecting "Exchange" as the reason
  2. Choose your preferred size/color
  3. Schedule pickup of the original item
  4. Receive the exchanged item within 5-7 business days after pickup

If the desired size/color is not available, you can opt for a full refund or store credit.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Innerwear, lingerie, and swimwear
  • Socks and hosiery
  • Customized or personalized items
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Items with missing tags or damaged packaging
  • Used or altered items
  • Jewelry (for hygiene reasons)
  • Gift cards

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  2. Share photos of the damaged item and packaging
  3. We will arrange a free pickup and replacement/refund

For damaged items, full shipping charges will be refunded along with the product amount.
